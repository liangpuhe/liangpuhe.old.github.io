Portfolio -- Campus App design

My friends and I found limitation of current available social softwares, which are short in fulfilling real-life social needs.People have few chance to know further or make friends with neighbors, but they are more reliable and dependable buddies.

So we decided to build and try out a location-based social App in our campus, linking online users with same purpose and proposing off-line meet-ups.

Key Considerations

In the end, we decided to use comment as communication tool, and kept privacy setting when owner creates an event. User is also able to leave comments under another user's homepage.


Event creator is able to chose one time spot or one period for an event. If period is chosen, a frequency slider shows up, and the creator chooses a general meeting frequency, so that new comer could easily identify the activity intensity by color and decide whether to join or not.
