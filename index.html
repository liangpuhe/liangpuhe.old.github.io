Portfolio -- Campus APP design

My friends and I found limitation of current available social softwares, which are short in fulfilling real-life social needs.We have few chance to know further or make friends with our neighbors, but we know they are more reliable and dependable buddies than people on network.

So we decided to build and try out a location-based social APP in our campus, linking online users with same purpose and proposing off-line meet-ups.
